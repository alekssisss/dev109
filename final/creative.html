My Hobby

My hobbies are playing the guitar and singing. I studied in a vocal studio for 10 years and after the start of quarantine in 2019, I learned to play the guitar myself. Since then I have been publishing short videos on the Internet in which I play and sing. It brings me a lot of pleasure, I've met a lot of people who really support me. In the future I would like to record my own song, as music has become a very important part of me.
